What is a great TV experience
for the newly defined couch potatoes?

Overview

The role of Smart TV needs to be redefined as the users' behavior pattern is intergrated. So we outlined smart TV's meaning as an integrated contents platform offering IoT services for user's living environment as well as multimedia contents. Also we designed a hardware interface of the remote controller with the intuitive interaction principles.

Goal

Setting An Identity and Experience structure for Smart TV within its environment

Role

User Researcher, UX Designer, GUI Designer

Discover - User and Market Research
TV started to contain contents which have been consumed through mobile

As the mobile first experience becomes familiar and dominant to users, the importance of TV media devices and user loyalty or reliance goes relatively low. The experience of contents consumption on mobile is also naturally reflected in TV. Therefore we were able to find out that user controllability of TV contents was expanded by providing real-time, possible to archive and interactive features together.

A fox with a bottleneck

But only with changing the content itself and changing the way it is delivered users can't expect they would get a proper user experience. We should take into account the characteristics of the media and the context of media usage so that we can define the structure of the user experience in a clever way.

In order to understand how the content consumption experience are occurred, contextual interview and diary studies were conducted targeting for smart TV users and set-top box users such as Chromecast, Xbox, and Roku. Those methodology were picked as a preliminary survey to picture out general and contextual behaviors of users so I could discover the meaning of TV itself in its environment as well as the direction of the way its experience structure should follow.

1. Lean-forward Users

Our first discovery was TV users now have active and passionate about what they want to do with TV, called 'Lean-forward Users'. The range and amount of content that TV can contain have certainly expanded. Lean-forward experience means that the viewer's attitude leans toward the TV rather than the leaned back. This means that users are actively doing something like searching for keywords, browsing categories, or storing content. However, while the content that TV can hold has expanded, the way of dealing with the content has not been followed up yet, so the users were feeling inconvenience, which is shown in the next discovery.
